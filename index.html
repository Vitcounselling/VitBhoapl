Instructions & Guidelines

  1. Candidates are advised to use the latest version of Google Chrome or Mozilla Firefox for a smooth counselling experience.
  2. This is a centralized counselling process for admission to B.Tech programmes offered at VIT Group of Institutions, namely Vellore, Chennai, VIT-AP, and VIT-Bhopal campuses. Based on their rank, eligible candidates may select their preferred campus, programme, and fee category during the counselling process.
  3. Candidates must login to the counselling portal using their Application Number, password and e-mail registered during the VITEEE application process.
  4. Candidates must not share their login credentials with anyone to ensure the security and confidentiality of their application.
